How to Choose the Right Dumbbells

By GymDude • Updated Jan 30, 2026

Fixed vs adjustable, grip comfort, and weight range explained for beginners.

  • If you train in a small room, adjustable dumbbells save space.
  • Check handle knurling—too aggressive can tear hands, too smooth can slip.
  • A starting range of 2.5–25 kg covers most beginner to intermediate needs.
